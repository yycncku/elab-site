Principal Investigator實驗室主持人

Alex Yu-Yu Chang

張佑宇
Professor教授 Director · IIM國經所 所長 NCKU國立成功大學

Dr. Alex Yu-Yu Chang is a Professor at the Institute of International Management, the Cross College Elite Program, and the Institute of Creative Industries Design at National Cheng Kung University, Taiwan. He directs the Institute of International Management and serves as Executive Secretary of the Chinese Professional Management Association of Tainan. He received his doctorate from the Graduate Institute of Technology Management, National Chung Hsing University, and held visiting scholar positions at RMIT University's Blockchain Innovation Hub (2020) and School of Economics, Finance and Marketing (2018). His research focuses on entrepreneurship, entrepreneurship education, user innovation behavior, and the governance of innovation commons.

張佑宇老師為國立成功大學國際經營管理研究所、全校不分系學士學位學程與創意產業設計研究所專任教授，並擔任國經所所長以及台南市企業經理協進會總幹事。2015 年自國立中興大學科技管理研究所取得博士學位，曾擔任澳洲墨爾本皇家理工大學（RMIT）區塊鏈創新中心（2020）與經濟金融行銷學院（2018）訪問學者。研究關注創業家精神、創業教育、使用者創新行為與創新公共財治理等議題。
